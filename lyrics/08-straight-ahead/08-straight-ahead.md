# Straight Ahead

**Song Number:** 08
**Target Date:** July 20, 2026
**Scripture Source:** Proverbs 4:20-27 _(personal reference — not published)_
**Genre:** Hip-Hop / Spoken Word over Lo-Fi Beats

---

### Verse 1

Eyes forward — that's the only rule I wrote
on the bathroom mirror back when I was losing hope.
Every notification pulling at my peripheral,
every shortcut promising the pinnacle —
nah, I've been down those crooked lanes before,
came back with empty pockets and a bruise I can't ignore.

My heart's a vault and everything I do pours out the door,
so I gotta watch the combination — can't leave it on the floor.
Left foot, right foot, straight ahead,
no detour, no deviation from the path I've set.

### Chorus

Guard it — guard it with everything,
'cause everything you are flows from this one spring.
Eyes ahead, don't turn left, don't lean right,
keep the road between the ditches through the longest night.
Straight ahead, straight ahead —
watch what you let in, watch what you let in.

### Verse 2

I used to swerve for every pretty distraction,
every voice that promised satisfaction in a fraction
of the time it takes to build something that lasts.
But the fast lane empties out fast.

Now I pay attention to the words I let in close,
the ones that heal the body, put the marrow in the bones.
I tilt my ear toward the signal, not the noise —
discipline ain't silence, it's a deliberate choice.

My feet don't turn to swerving avenues no more,
I removed the crooked lip, put the truth back at the core.

### Chorus

Guard it — guard it with everything,
'cause everything you are flows from this one spring.
Eyes ahead, don't turn left, don't lean right,
keep the road between the ditches through the longest night.
Straight ahead, straight ahead —
watch what you let in, watch what you let in.

### Bridge

_(spoken)_
They'll tell you the scenic route is living —
but the scenic route is just a pretty way of drifting.
I'm not rigid, I'm just rooted,
I'm not narrow, I'm just pointed.
Every crooked path I ever walked
taught me the value of a straight line.

### Final Chorus

Guard it — guard it like it's everything,
'cause everything you love flows from this one spring.
Look straight, walk straight, speak straight tonight,
keep the road between the ditches and you'll walk into the light.
Straight ahead, straight ahead —
I'm watching every door, I'm watching every door.

### Outro

Left foot. Right foot.
Straight ahead.
