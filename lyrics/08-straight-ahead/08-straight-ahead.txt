Straight Ahead

Hip-Hop / Spoken Word over Lo-Fi Beats

[Verse 1]

Eyes forward - that was the only law I wrote,
on a fogged-up mirror when I almost lost my hope.
Every buzz kept tugging at my side vision,
every shortcut sold me counterfeit precision.
I know those crooked lanes, I've paid that fare,
came home with empty hands and damage everywhere.

My heart's a spring, and everything runs out,
so I guard the source before it floods with doubt.
Left foot, right foot, straight ahead,
no drift, no ditch, no trading truth for bread.

[Hook]

Guard it — guard it with everything,
'cause everything you are flows from this one spring.
Eyes ahead, don't turn left, don't lean right,
keep the road between the ditches through the longest night.
Straight ahead, straight ahead —
watch what you let in, watch what you let in.

[Verse 2]

I used to swerve for every polished distraction,
every voice that packaged peace in instant action.
But what is rushed collapses just as fast,
and empty speed can never build what lasts.

Now I weigh every word before it enters close,
the kind that heals the flesh and puts strength in bones.
I tune my ear to wisdom, not the crowd,
discipline is not mute - it's truth out loud.

My feet don't flirt with sideways roads no more,
I stripped the crooked speech and set truth at the core.
