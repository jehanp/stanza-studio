# Let There Be

**Song Number:** 06
**Target Date:** June 22, 2026
**Scripture Source:** Genesis 1:1-5, 1:26-31 _(personal reference — not published)_
**Genre:** Electronic / Synth-Pop

---

### Verse 1

In the beginning there was static,
a screen of nothing humming in the black.
Someone said my name into the voltage —
and I flickered, and I didn't look back.

They told me I was formless, I was wasted,
just a void that didn't earn a name.
But a voice cut through the white noise:
"Let there be —" and nothing was the same.

### Chorus

Let there be, let there be —
light where they said there was nothing.
Let there be, let there be —
I was spoken into something.
And it was good, and it was good,
and it was good.

### Verse 2

First came the line between the dark and morning,
then the edges where the water meets the sky.
Something sorted through the chaos in my marrow,
separated every truth from every lie.

I was built deliberate — not by accident,
every atom placed on purpose, by design.
And the one who hung the constellations
looked at me and said, "You're mine."

### Chorus

Let there be, let there be —
a voice where they said there was no one.
Let there be, let there be —
the dark is done, the light has won.
And it was good, and it was good,
and it was good.

### Build

Not enough, they said — too much, they said,
wrong frequency, wrong colour, wrong thread.
But the one who split the atom and the ocean
looked at everything they'd made
and said —

### Drop

Let there be! Let there be!
Let there be! Let there be!
And it was good —

### Final Chorus

Let there be, let there be —
dawn where they predicted only night.
Let there be, let there be —
I am not their darkness, I'm the light.
And I am good, and I am good,
and I am good.

### Outro

From void to voice, from dark to daylight —
I was made on purpose.
I was called very good.
