Alternative Rock / Post-Punk Revival

[Verse 1]

They said the distance would dissolve us,
said the silence would corrode the wire.
They threw the worst they had — the letters,
the lawyers, the midnight kitchen fire.

I watched the ceiling cave in February,
counted every scar across my chest.
They whispered, "It's already over" —
I whispered back, "You haven't seen the rest."

[Pre-Chorus]

Every single thing that tried to break us
got folded into something we could use.

[Chorus]

Nothing can, nothing can —
not the grave, not the distance,
not the dark, not the silence,
not the powers, not the plans.
Nothing can, nothing can —
you could stack the whole world on this love
and it would stand.

[Verse 2]

Not height, not depth, not what's above me,
not the future hammering the door.
Not the angel or the principality,
not the fear I've never named before.

I was called before I had an answer,
justified before I had a case.
Whatever comes has already been outrun
by a love that knew my face.

[Pre-Chorus]

If the whole thing's working toward a purpose,
then the wreckage has a reason underneath.

[Chorus]

Nothing can, nothing can —
not the grave, not the distance,
not the dark, not the silence,
not the powers, not the plans.
Nothing can, nothing can —
you could burn the whole house down around this love
and it would stand.

[Bridge]

I am convinced — not hoping, not wishing —
I am convinced like a closing argument.
No height, no depth, no thing in all creation
could redact the covenant.

[Final Chorus]

Nothing can, nothing can —
not the death, not the living,
not the loss, not the winning,
not the start, not the end.
Nothing can, nothing can —
you could throw every weapon ever forged at us
and love would stand.

[Outro]

Nothing. Nothing. Nothing can.
