Wings

Cinematic Pop / Anthemic

[Verse 1]

I lay out on the rooftop when the city went to sleep,
counted all the stars above until the dark turned deep.
The constellations mapped a grief I couldn't navigate —
I read them with wide-open eyes and couldn't lift my own weight.

[Verse 2]

They said find your north star, chart your course, begin again,
but my eyes had lost the language of direction and of then.
So I lay there on the concrete like a dare,
and the silence underneath those stars was prayer.

[Pre-Chorus]

I stopped running,
I stopped climbing,
I let the ground remember how to hold me.

[Chorus]

And I felt it rise — something underneath,
a light beyond the far side of the dark and past belief.
I'm not falling anymore, I'm not grinding in the rust —
I'm lifting off the pavement on a strength that isn't us.
Wings, wings —
I didn't grow them, but they're carrying me.

[Verse 3]

The ones who chase the light burn out before the dawn,
but there's a slower kind of shining for the ones who carry on.
I waited on the rooftop like a fool beneath the stars —
turns out it doesn't matter when the light breaks through the dark.

[Pre-Chorus]

I stopped striving,
I stopped proving,
I let the stillness do the moving.

[Chorus]

And I felt it rise — something underneath,
a light beyond the far side of the dark and past belief.
I'm not crawling anymore, I'm not drowning in the flood —
I'm running down the highway on a pulse that's in my blood.
Wings, wings —
I didn't grow them, but they're carrying me.

[Bridge]

They say the stars don't tire of shining,
they say the dark won't last the night —
but I have lain beneath their keeping,
and I'm still here, still reaching for the light.

[Final Chorus]

And I feel it rise — something underneath,
the everlasting arms of a love beyond belief.
I'm not weary anymore, I'm not fainting on the road —
I'm soaring past the rooftops and I'm shedding every load.
Wings, wings —
I didn't earn them, but they're carrying me.
