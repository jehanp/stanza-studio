Wings

Cinematic Pop / Anthemic

[Verse 1]

I sat at an empty station when the last train pulled away,
stared at the departures board until the letters turned to grey.
The platform had a silence louder than the rails —
I read it in my hollow chest and let it fill my sails.

[Verse 2]

They said run and catch the next one, you've still got another shot,
but my legs forgot the language of the stride and of the slot.
So I sat there on the bench like a dare,
and the stillness on that platform — it was there.

[Pre-Chorus]

I stopped running,
I stopped climbing,
I let the ground remember how to hold me.

[Chorus]

And I felt it rise — something underneath,
a wind inside the station lifting higher than my grief.
I'm not falling anymore, I'm not grinding in the rust —
I'm lifting off the pavement on a strength that isn't us.
Wings, wings —
I didn't grow them, but they're carrying me.

[Verse 3]

The ones who sprint from gate to gate burn bright and miss their ride,
but there's a quieter kind of journey for the ones who wait inside.
I sat there on the platform like a fool who missed his train —
turns out it doesn't matter when the wind comes back again.

[Pre-Chorus]

I stopped striving,
I stopped proving,
I let the stillness do the moving.

[Chorus]

And I felt it rise — something underneath,
a wind inside the station lifting higher than my grief.
I'm not crawling anymore, I'm not drowning in the flood —
I'm running down the highway on a pulse that's in my blood.
Wings, wings —
I didn't grow them, but they're carrying me.

[Bridge]

They say the train comes round again,
they say the schedule holds — but then
I have missed the one I needed and been carried to a better end,
and I'm still here, still standing, still a friend.

[Final Chorus]

And I feel it rise — something underneath,
the everlasting arms of a love beyond belief.
I'm not weary anymore, I'm not fainting on the road —
I'm soaring past the rooftops and I'm shedding every load.
Wings, wings —
I didn't earn them, but they're carrying me.
