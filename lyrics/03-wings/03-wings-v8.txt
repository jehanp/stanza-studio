Wings

Cinematic Pop / Anthemic

[Verse 1]

I collapsed inside the greenhouse where the last things try to grow,
face down in the potting soil with nowhere left to go.
The roots around me twisted in a pattern like a map —
I traced the lines with trembling hands and couldn't find my way back.

[Verse 2]

They said water more and tend it, give it light and give it room,
but my hands forgot the language of the seed and of the bloom.
So I sat there in the soil like a dare,
and the dark beneath the roots was teaching me to bear.

[Pre-Chorus]

I stopped running,
I stopped climbing,
I let the ground remember how to hold me.

[Chorus]

And I felt it rise — something underneath,
a green thing pushing upward out of drought and out of grief.
I'm not falling anymore, I'm not grinding in the rust —
I'm lifting off the pavement on a strength that isn't us.
Wings, wings —
I didn't grow them, but they're carrying me.

[Verse 3]

The ones who force the flower lose the fruit they'll never find,
but there's a slower kind of blooming for the ones who take their time.
I waited in the greenhouse like a seed still under snow —
turns out it doesn't matter how long growing goes.

[Pre-Chorus]

I stopped striving,
I stopped proving,
I let the stillness do the moving.

[Chorus]

And I felt it rise — something underneath,
a green thing pushing upward out of drought and out of grief.
I'm not crawling anymore, I'm not drowning in the flood —
I'm running down the highway on a pulse that's in my blood.
Wings, wings —
I didn't grow them, but they're carrying me.

[Bridge]

They say the seed knows when to break,
they say the root knows where to take —
but I have lain in fallow ground and been forgotten,
and I'm still here, still wide awake.

[Final Chorus]

And I feel it rise — something underneath,
the everlasting arms of a love beyond belief.
I'm not weary anymore, I'm not fainting on the road —
I'm soaring past the rooftops and I'm shedding every load.
Wings, wings —
I didn't earn them, but they're carrying me.
