Wings

Cinematic Pop / Anthemic

[Verse 1]

I ran until the desert swallowed what was left of me,
collapsed beneath a canyon wall where no one else could see.
The sandstone held a layering that looked like time itself —
I read it with my eyes and couldn't lift myself.

[Verse 2]

They said try harder, reach further, don't give in,
but my sinews had forgotten how to pull and how to win.
So I lay there in the silence like a dare,
and the silence wasn't hollow — it had known me there.

[Pre-Chorus]

I stopped running,
I stopped climbing,
I let the ground remember how to hold me.

[Chorus]

And I felt it rise — something underneath,
an ember in the canyon lifting higher than belief.
I'm not falling anymore, I'm not grinding in the rust —
I'm lifting off the pavement on a strength that isn't us.
Wings, wings —
I didn't grow them, but they're carrying me.

[Verse 3]

The ones who race burn bright and crater fast,
but there's a slower kind of strength for those who choose to last.
I waited in the desert like a fool or like a root —
turns out it doesn't matter when there's fruit.

[Pre-Chorus]

I stopped striving,
I stopped proving,
I let the stillness do the moving.

[Chorus]

And I felt it rise — something underneath,
an ember in the canyon lifting higher than belief.
I'm not crawling anymore, I'm not drowning in the flood —
I'm running down the highway on a pulse that's in my blood.
Wings, wings —
I didn't grow them, but they're carrying me.

[Bridge]

They say the sinews won't be severed,
they say the ancient arms hold firm —
but I have fallen and been carried,
and I'm still here, and still I learn.

[Final Chorus]

And I feel it rise — something underneath,
the ancient arms of a love beyond belief.
I'm not weary anymore, I'm not fainting on the road —
I'm soaring past the rooftops and I'm shedding every load.
Wings, wings —
I didn't earn them, but they're carrying me.
