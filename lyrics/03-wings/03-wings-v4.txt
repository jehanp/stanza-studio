Wings

Cinematic Pop / Anthemic

[Verse 1]

I ran until the frost had claimed the last of what I had,
collapsed beneath bare branches in the long November sad.
The bark had cracked in patterns that looked like something knew —
I read it with my eyes and couldn't find my way through.

[Verse 2]

They said keep burning, push the embers, don't go cold,
but my fire had forgotten what it meant to take a hold.
So I sat there in the frost like a dare,
and the frost was not my enemy — it was rare.

[Pre-Chorus]

I stopped running,
I stopped climbing,
I let the ground remember how to hold me.

[Chorus]

And I felt it rise — something underneath,
a slow burn in the frost lifting higher than belief.
I'm not falling anymore, I'm not grinding in the rust —
I'm lifting off the pavement on a strength that isn't us.
Wings, wings —
I didn't grow them, but they're carrying me.

[Verse 3]

The ones who burn at full flame crater fast,
but there's a slow and lasting fire for the ones who choose to last.
I waited in the ashes like a fool who planted seed —
turns out the frost will carry you to everything you need.

[Pre-Chorus]

I stopped striving,
I stopped proving,
I let the stillness do the moving.

[Chorus]

And I felt it rise — something underneath,
a slow burn in the frost lifting higher than belief.
I'm not crawling anymore, I'm not drowning in the flood —
I'm running down the highway on a pulse that's in my blood.
Wings, wings —
I didn't grow them, but they're carrying me.

[Bridge]

They say the frost won't stop the living,
they say the bare branch blooms in spring —
but I have sat beneath those branches,
and I'm still here, still marveling.

[Final Chorus]

And I feel it rise — something underneath,
the everlasting arms of a love beyond belief.
I'm not weary anymore, I'm not fainting on the road —
I'm soaring past the rooftops and I'm shedding every load.
Wings, wings —
I didn't earn them, but they're carrying me.
