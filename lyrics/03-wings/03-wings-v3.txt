Wings

Cinematic Pop / Anthemic

[Verse 1]

I swam until the ocean had the better end of me,
sank below the current where the light had ceased to be.
The surface held a shimmer that looked like another world —
I watched it from below with every finger curled.

[Verse 2]

They said keep swimming, don't go under, fight the tide,
but my arms had lost the memory of the other side.
So I floated in the silence like a dare,
and the current wasn't cruel — it was there.

[Pre-Chorus]

I stopped running,
I stopped climbing,
I let the ground remember how to hold me.

[Chorus]

And I felt it rise — something underneath,
a current I had not sought lifting higher than belief.
I'm not falling anymore, I'm not grinding in the rust —
I'm lifting off the pavement on a strength that isn't us.
Wings, wings —
I didn't grow them, but they're carrying me.

[Verse 3]

The ones who fight the tide burn bright and tire fast,
but there's a drifting kind of grace for those who choose to last.
I floated in the current like a fool or like a fish —
turns out the water carries what we wish.

[Pre-Chorus]

I stopped striving,
I stopped proving,
I let the stillness do the moving.

[Chorus]

And I felt it rise — something underneath,
a current I had not sought lifting higher than belief.
I'm not crawling anymore, I'm not drowning in the flood —
I'm running down the highway on a pulse that's in my blood.
Wings, wings —
I didn't grow them, but they're carrying me.

[Bridge]

They say the tides will not consume us,
they say the current holds us true —
but I've been under and been lifted,
and I'm still here, still flowing through.

[Final Chorus]

And I feel it rise — something underneath,
the everlasting arms of a love beyond belief.
I'm not weary anymore, I'm not fainting on the road —
I'm soaring past the rooftops and I'm shedding every load.
Wings, wings —
I didn't earn them, but they're carrying me.
