Come Away

Neo-Soul / Jazzy Pop

[Verse 1]

I mortared shut the window in November,
told myself the cold was what I needed then.
Said no more warmth, no more light through curtains —
I'd learned enough from letting strangers in.

Then soft across the threshold came your calling,
low and sure as rainfall on the stone:
"The doves are back, the branches full of blossoms —
come out, my love, you've been too long alone."

[Pre-Chorus]

The vineyard's breathing sweet beneath the hillside,
the tender grapes are climbing toward the sun.
I hear a voice that's carried through the seasons —
the song of thaw has finally begun.

[Chorus]

Come away, come away —
the frost has gone, the dark has had its day.
Unbolt the grief inside your chest,
love is here to put you back to rest.
Come away, come away —
your hiding place has kept you long enough today.

[Verse 2]

I listened at the plaster for the garden,
the hum of soil waking after sleep.
The turtle doves were calling to each other
beneath a sky too wide and blue to keep.

You never asked me to explain the silence,
just waited in the warmth outside the door.
And something ancient in my chest remembered
what it felt like not to need a cure.

[Chorus]

Come away, come away —
the frost has gone, the dark has had its day.
Love was rooted at your gate,
you don't need to hesitate.
Come away, come away —
the meadow bloomed the whole time you were away.

[Bridge]

I was the permafrost beneath the mountain,
you were the stubborn seed that split the stone —
pressing through the cold weight of my wintering
until the smallest green thing found its home.

[Final Chorus]

Come away, come away —
I couldn't hold the season — it gave way.
I'm walking out past everything I feared —
love was never lost, love was always here.
Come away, come away —
I think I'm ready — I think I'm ready for the day.

[Vamp]

Come away... come away...
The door is open now — the door is open.
Come away... come away...

[Outro]

The rain is over, the rain is gone —
the morning smells like moving on.
