Come Away

Neo-Soul / Jazzy Pop

[Verse 1]

I sealed the door with all the things I carried —
grief stacked high as I could stack the stones.
Swore I'd stay in silence rather than risk
the particular kind of pain of being known.

But there you stood where lamplight meets the evening,
your voice a thing the darkness couldn't hold:
"The winter's broken, vines are thick with perfume —
come out, come see — the world has all turned gold."

[Pre-Chorus]

The fig tree's heavy, bending with its giving,
the little foxes gone from off the vine.
The birds are weaving morning into music —
a hymn I thought I'd never hear in time.

[Chorus]

Come away, come away —
the ice is gone, surrender to the day.
Pry open what you've kept locked tight,
love has walked the long road through the night.
Come away, come away —
the life you've missed is just outside today.

[Verse 2]

I pressed my face against the cold tile, listening —
heard green things breathe beneath the thawing mud.
The doves were perched in pairs along the rooftop
beneath a sky scrubbed clean of winter's blood.

You didn't push, you didn't make demands —
just stood there like a constant, like a fact.
And something I had buried in my marrow
began to surface — whole, intact.

[Chorus]

Come away, come away —
the ice is gone, surrender to the day.
Love kept vigil at your wall,
you don't have to guard it all.
Come away, come away —
the blossom came regardless of your stay.

[Bridge]

I was the hardened soil beneath the snowline,
you were the rain that knew exactly where to fall —
wearing through the layers of my frozen self
until the buried seed could hear the call.

[Final Chorus]

Come away, come away —
the wheel has turned and I can't make it stay.
I'm stepping through the threshold I once built —
love was never blame, love carries no guilt.
Come away, come away —
I think I'm ready — I think I'm ready for the day.

[Vamp]

Come away... come away...
The door is open now — the door is open.
Come away... come away...

[Outro]

The rain is over, the rain is gone —
the morning smells like moving on.
