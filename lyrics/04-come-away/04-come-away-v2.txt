Come Away

Neo-Soul / Jazzy Pop

[Verse 1]
I sealed the gate in January,
packed the earth down, cold and wary.
Buried every tender thing beneath the stone —
let the roots press down and grieve alone.

But there you stood at the edge of the dig,
your voice unearthing something small and big:
"The strata's shifting, love, below your feet —
come see what's rising through the buried heat."

[Pre-Chorus]
The marrow of the frozen ground
is pressing up what grief had found.
The roots push through the sealed stone ring —
a buried thing remembering.

[Chorus]
Come away, come away —
the winter's gone, the grey gave way.
Open up your bolted chest,
love has come to give you rest.
Come away, come away —
you've been hiding long enough today.

[Verse 2]
I pressed my hand against the excavation wall
and felt the heartbeat of the ancient hall.
The amber light held what was stored below —
a warmth the frozen layers couldn't know.

You didn't chisel, didn't pry the seam,
you held the lantern steady like a dream.
And something sealed inside my bones
remembered what it means to come back home.

[Chorus]
Come away, come away —
the winter's gone, the grey gave way.
Love was patient at your door,
you don't have to hide no more.
Come away, come away —
the flowers came while you were away.

[Bridge]
I was the sealed and salted ground,
you were the root that pressed its way through stone I'd found —
boring through the grief of me
to let the deepest marrow free.

[Final Chorus]
Come away, come away —
the season turned, I couldn't stay.
I'm stepping out beyond the wall I've made —
love was never far, love never strayed.
Come away, come away —
I think I'm ready for the day.

[Vamp]
Come away... come away...
The door is open now — the door is open.
Come away... come away...

[Outro]
The dig is over, the earth is warm —
the morning smells like something new is born.
