Come Away

Neo-Soul / Jazzy Pop

[Verse 1]

I bricked the doorway up in autumn,
swept the threshold cold and solemn.
Told myself I didn't need the rain —
let the empty rooms absorb the pain.

But there you hummed outside the hedge row low,
your voice like honey on a ember's glow:
"The rains have passed, the sky is clean and wide —
come walk with me, there's nothing left to hide."

[Pre-Chorus]

The tender vine has found the sun,
the fig tree heavy, season done.
I hear the doves in pairs above —
the world is thick with waking love.

[Chorus]

Come away, come away —
the winter's gone, the grey gave way.
Open up your bolted chest,
love has come to give you rest.
Come away, come away —
you've been hiding long enough today.

[Verse 2]

I sat inside the hollow quiet
and felt the garden break its riot.
The blossoms pressed against the pane —
the earth insisting on its name.

You sang so soft outside the stone
until the silence felt less alone.
And something rusted in my chest
unlocked and finally came to rest.

[Chorus]

Come away, come away —
the winter's gone, the grey gave way.
Love was patient at your door,
you don't have to hide no more.
Come away, come away —
the flowers came while you were away.

[Bridge]

I was the shuttered, dusty hall,
you were the season past the wall —
arriving green through every crack
to call the sleeping garden back.

[Final Chorus]

Come away, come away —
the season turned, I couldn't stay.
I'm stepping out beyond the wall I've made —
love was never far, love never strayed.
Come away, come away —
I think I'm ready for the day.

[Vamp]

Come away... come away...
The door is open now — the door is open.
Come away... come away...

[Outro]

The rain is over, the rain is gone —
the morning smells like moving on.
