Come Away

Neo-Soul / Jazzy Pop

[Verse 1]

I bolted down the cellar against the sky —
the drought was long, the ground was cracked and dry.
I drew what little water still remained
and told the cracked earth I could live without the rain.

But then I heard your voice above the floor —
a soft and steady knocking at the door:
"The sky has broken open, let it through —
come on up, beloved, the cistern's filling new."

[Pre-Chorus]

The canyon walls are blooming after dry,
the swallows trace their circles in the sky.
The vineyard row is greener than it's been —
the cistern sings with something cold and clean.

[Chorus]

Come away, come away —
the drought has lost its hold today.
The cistern rings with something clear —
come up and drink, there's nothing left to fear.
Come away, come away —
the sky broke open — here to stay.

[Verse 2]

I pressed my ear against the cellar door
and heard the rain drum on the hardpan floor,
the water running down the canyon stone,
the roots cracking through where I had felt alone.

And you had not gone far from where you stood —
you sat beside that well the way love could.
You held the rope down where I'd find my way,
patient as the river, calm as day.

[Chorus]

Come away, come away —
the cellar dark has had its say.
The cracked earth split to let the green come through —
come up and see what rain and love can do.
Come away, come away —
the well holds more than winter's grey.

[Bridge]

I was the dried-up bed of a cracked creek,
you were the rain that came back week by week —
wearing through the hardpan of my thirsty years
until the deepest roots could reach the clear.

[Final Chorus]

Come away, come away —
the long and sunbaked season fell away.
I'm stepping out from underground and stone —
love was never drought, love was never alone.
Come away, come away —
I think I'm ready — I think I'm ready for the day.

[Vamp]

Come away... come away...
The door is open now — the door is open.
Come away... come away...

[Outro]

The rain is over, the rain is gone —
the morning smells like moving on.
