Come Away

Neo-Soul / Jazzy Pop

[Verse 1]

I shuttered every window facing out to sea
and let the fog come in and swallow me.
I made my room a colourless, still place
and told the harbour it could not reach my face.

But you came through the grey and called my name —
your voice cut through the fog like rising flame:
"The lighthouse found the shoreline, love — again —
come on out, beloved, the fog is lifting then."

[Pre-Chorus]

The white gulls wheel and cry above the bay,
the sea-rose on the cliffside blooms today.
The vineyard row breathes salt along the hill —
the tide is running in and running still.

[Chorus]

Come away, come away —
the fog has rolled back off the bay.
The lighthouse swept the dark to find your shore —
come stand beside me where you stood before.
Come away, come away —
the sea has cleared a path today.

[Verse 2]

I pressed against the shutters hard to hear
the ocean working through the fog out there —
the foghorn calling, steady, like a vow,
a sound that held the sea and kept it now.

You hadn't moved from standing on the shore,
you waited while I bolted every door.
Your voice like breaking waves that always come —
steady as the lighthouse, patient, warm.

[Chorus]

Come away, come away —
the fog has sailed out past the reef.
The morning gives the shoreline back its light —
come walk with me and love will come out right.
Come away, come away —
the wide sky holds more than your grief.

[Bridge]

I was the fog that swallowed up the coast,
you were the light that found me when I needed most —
burning through the grey of all my shuttered years
until the harbour opened past my fears.

[Final Chorus]

Come away, come away —
the grey and fogbound season fell away.
I'm stepping out from shuttered rooms to light —
love was never fog, love burns through night.
Come away, come away —
I think I'm ready — I think I'm ready for the day.

[Vamp]

Come away... come away...
The door is open now — the door is open.
Come away... come away...

[Outro]

The rain is over, the rain is gone —
the morning smells like moving on.
