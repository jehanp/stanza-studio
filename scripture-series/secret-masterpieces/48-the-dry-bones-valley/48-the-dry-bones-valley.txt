The Dry Bones Valley

Electronic / Industrial Gospel

[Verse 1]
The valley stretches white with ancient bones,
and every rib is bleaching on the stones.
How long have they been lying in this place,
without a breath of wind upon their face?

[Verse 2]
He asked me: can these dry bones live again?
I said: you know the answer — I don't pretend.
The question wasn't doubt, it was a dare,
to prophesy to death and see who's there.

[Chorus]
Speak to the bones and watch the sinew crawl,
and tell the dead to stand up, tell them all.
The breath is coming from the four wild winds,
and what was over starts again, begins.

[Verse 3]
I spoke and heard the rattling underground,
as bone found bone with that unearthly sound.
The flesh crept back like morning over hills,
but still no breath — just bodies, cold and still.

[Chorus]
Speak to the bones and watch the sinew crawl,
and tell the dead to stand up, tell them all.
The breath is coming from the four wild winds,
and what was over starts again, begins.

[Bridge]
Prophesy to the wind — come from the four,
and breathe upon the slain across this floor.
The army that was nothing but decay,
is standing on its feet and facing day.

[Final Chorus]
Speak to the bones — they're listening for your voice,
the dead are not beyond a second choice.
The breath has entered everything that fell,
and what was once a graveyard is now well.

[Outro]
A valley full of endings found its breath,
and nothing is too dead to conquer death.
