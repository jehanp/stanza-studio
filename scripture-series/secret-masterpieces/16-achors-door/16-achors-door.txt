Achor's Door

Gothic Folk / Dark Americana

[Verse 1]
Where the dust is rising and the road is dry,
under a low and iron-colored sky,
the quiet wind is blowing through the gate,
to tell me everything I left can wait.

[Chorus]
The valley of the broken stone is wide,
with nothing but the desert left inside.
But look closely where the mountain splits in two,
a little door of hope is coming through.

[Verse 2]
I thought the wilderness was just a place
to hide the cold expression of my face.
But here the sand is soft and speaks my name,
and takes away the heavy shoes of shame.

[Chorus]
The valley of the broken stone is wide,
with nothing but the desert left inside.
But look closely where the mountain splits in two,
a little door of hope is coming through.

[Bridge]
We dig for vineyards in the hardest clay,
and find the water that they threw away.
The place we thought was only built for death,
is breathing in and giving back our breath.

[Final Chorus]
The valley of the broken stone is wide,
with nothing but the desert left inside.
But look closely where the mountain splits in two,
the door is open and the light is new.

[Outro]
The heavy door is swinging wide and clear,
the wilderness is singing very near.
