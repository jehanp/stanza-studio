The Wind of the Valley

Cinematic Indie Rock / Power Ballad

[Verse 1]
The valley is a graveyard of our plans,
and everything we built with human hands.
The timber has been turned to dusty grey,
and all the heavy stones have slipped away.

[Verse 2]
The dry and silent bones are on the ground,
without a breath of music or of sound.
We lived in this defeat for many years,
and watered all the desert with our tears.

[Chorus]
But listen to the rustle in the trees,
a whisper turning slowly to a breeze.
So pull the dry and frozen air inside,
where nothing but the silence used to hide.

[Verse 3]
The dust begins to settle on the floor,
and someone stands beside the open door.
A warmth is moving quickly through the space,
and showing us the outlines of a face.

[Chorus]
But listen to the rustle in the trees,
a whisper turning slowly to a breeze.
So pull the dry and frozen air inside,
where nothing but the silence used to hide.

[Bridge]
From north and south and east and western sky,
the mighty breath is coming drawing nigh.
It fills the empty ribs with living song,
and makes the broken spirits very strong.

[Final Chorus]
So listen to the thunder in the trees,
the storm of life is rising on the breeze.
We pull the living wind inside our chest,
and feel the heavy shadow set to rest.

[Outro]
The valley of the dead began to sing,
and winter has been swallowed by the spring.
