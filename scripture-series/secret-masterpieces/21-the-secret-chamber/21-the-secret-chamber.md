# The Secret Chamber

**Song Number:** 21
**Target Date:** August 17, 2026
**Scripture Source:** Isaiah 26:20 / Song of Solomon 2:4 _(personal reference — not published)_
**Genre:** Ethereal Dream Pop / Synth Folk

---

### Verse 1

The wind is beating on the window pane,
and washes down the cold and heavy rain.
The thunder rumbles on the empty street,
and shakes the gravel underneath my feet.

### Pre-Chorus

But inside here the candle has a glow,
and is the only light we need to know.
The heavy lock is turned inside the door,
and we can leave the worry on the floor.

### Chorus

Inside the secret chamber we can hide,
and let the angry world remain outside.
A quiet room where nothing else can squeeze,
and we can find a little bit of ease.

### Verse 2

They scream of panic and they scream of fight,
and try to make us tremble in the night.
They draft their notices of fear and shame,
and call us by the oldest, broken name.

### Pre-Chorus

But inside here the candle has a glow,
and is the only light we need to know.
The heavy lock is turned inside the door,
and we can leave the worry on the floor.

### Chorus

Inside the secret chamber we can hide,
and let the angry world remain outside.
A quiet room where nothing else can squeeze,
and we can find a little bit of ease.

### Bridge

The storm can wear itself away to dust,
for we have found the only one we trust.
And here inside the silent, healing space,
we find the outline of a gentle face.

### Final Chorus

Inside the secret chamber we can hide,
and let the angry world remain outside.
A quiet room where nothing else can squeeze,
where we are gathered underneath the breeze.

### Outro

The locks are tight and we are safe and dry,
until the flashing storm has left the sky.
