# The Engraved Hand

**Song Number:** 28
**Target Date:** November 23, 2026
**Scripture Source:** Isaiah 49:15-16 _(personal reference — not published)_
**Genre:** Shoegaze / Ambient Pop

---

### Verse 1

The windows rattle in the empty place,
where rain has washed away your quiet face.
I wander through the rooms of cold and stone,
and feel as if I'm permanently on my own.

### Verse 2

Did you forget the promise that you made,
when all the summer flowers start to fade?
Are we forgotten like a broken door,
that nobody will look at anymore?

### Chorus

But look, the keeper's hand is open wide,
with marks that never can be washed aside.
Your face is cut into the open skin,
where all the ancient memory can begin.

### Verse 3

A mother might forget her nursing child,
and let her heart run empty in the wild.
But I will never lose you in the dark,
or cover up the deep and heavy mark.

### Chorus

But look, the keeper's hand is open wide,
with marks that never can be washed aside.
Your face is cut into the open skin,
where all the ancient memory can begin.

### Bridge

The walls are standing always in the sight,
and guarded in the middle of the night.
An outline that behaves like steel and gold,
to keep us safe inside the master's fold.

### Final Chorus

So look, the keeper's hand has set you free,
and written names that outlive all the sea.
Your face is carved inside the open skin,
where everlasting morning can begin.

### Outro

Engraved and safe inside the open palm,
the rolling storm is settling to a calm.
