# The Morning Star Rising

**Song Number:** 25
**Target Date:** October 12, 2026
**Scripture Source:** 2 Peter 1:19 _(personal reference — not published)_
**Genre:** Synth-Wave / Electropop

---

### Verse 1

The darkness has been covering the town,
and wrapping everything in quiet brown.
The sun has been away for many years,
and left us in the valley of our fears.

### Pre-Chorus

But in my hand I hold a tiny spark,
a little flame that opens up the dark.
It doesn't reach the borders of the sky,
but keeps the hope from starting now to die.

### Chorus

A silent lamp inside a pitch-black place,
reflects the quiet outline of your face.
Wait for the morning star to start to rise,
and wake the sleepy circles of our eyes.

### Verse 2

They tell us that the morning is a myth,
and blow away the hopes we started with.
They sell us artificial glass and glow,
and say that is the only light to know.

### Pre-Chorus

But in my hand I hold a tiny spark,
a little flame that opens up the dark.
It doesn't reach the borders of the sky,
but keeps the hope from starting now to die.

### Chorus

A silent lamp inside a pitch-black place,
reflects the quiet outline of your face.
Wait for the morning star to start to rise,
and wake the sleepy circles of our eyes.

### Bridge

The blackest night will have to stretch and bend,
because the shadow isn't built to end.
The tiny candle flickering in here,
is burning out the remnants of my fear.

### Final Chorus

A silent lamp inside a pitch-black place,
has found the quiet outline of your face.
The morning star has started now to dawn,
and rises in our hearts when night is gone.

### Outro

The artificial neon starts to grey,
as something ancient ushers in the day.
