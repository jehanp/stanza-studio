# The Eagle's Nest

**Song Number:** 29
**Target Date:** December 7, 2026
**Scripture Source:** Deuteronomy 32:11 _(personal reference — not published)_
**Genre:** Cinematic Orchestral Pop

---

### Verse 1

The twigs are snapping under heavy blows,
as everything we knew begins to close.
The cozy walls are scattered in the gale,
to show us that our defenses are so frail.

### Verse 2

The home we loved has been disrupted down,
and thrown into the valley, dry and brown.
The keeper of the nest has made it clear,
that we cannot remain in comfort here.

### Chorus

And now we fall into the empty space,
with cold and biting wind upon our face.
But look, a golden wing is diving low,
to catch us where the storm begin to blow.

### Verse 3

I screamed in terror as the air went by,
and watched the empty borders of the sky.
I thought the fall was only built to kill,
and let my broken spirit have its fill.

### Chorus

And now we fall into the empty space,
with cold and biting wind upon our face.
But look, a golden wing is diving low,
to catch us where the storm begin to blow.

### Bridge

We rest upon the feathers wide and warm,
above the lightning and the heavy storm.
We learn to fly because the house was torn,
and find the rising of a longer dawn.

### Final Chorus

So let us soar inside the empty space,
with quiet freedom on our happy face.
The golden wing is lifting us so high,
to show us how to navigate the sky.

### Outro

The nest is gone but we have found our wings,
and rises high above all mortal things.
