# The Signet Ring

**Song Number:** 26
**Target Date:** October 26, 2026
**Scripture Source:** Haggai 2:23 / Song of Solomon 8:6 _(personal reference — not published)_
**Genre:** Dark Chamber Folk / Acoustic Cello

---

### Verse 1

The mountains are shaking and starting to fall,
and dust has been settling over the hall.
The palaces crumble and turn into clay,
and all of the kings have been carried away.

### Chorus

But you are engraved in the wax and the gold,
a secret that never was traded or sold.
When everything else has been shaken and down,
you lie like a seal on the edge of the crown.

### Verse 2

The papers are tear-stained and lost in the wind,
and all of the plans we had made have been thinned.
No signature stands on the parchment of old,
and even the hearths have been growing so cold.

### Chorus

But you are engraved in the wax and the gold,
a secret that never was traded or sold.
When everything else has been shaken and down,
you lie like a seal on the edge of the crown.

### Bridge

Pressed deep in the heart and pressed deep on the arm,
no fire can burn you and nothing can harm.
The signature mark of the maker is clear,
and wipes out the ashes of yesterday's fear.

### Final Chorus

For you are engraved in the wax and the gold,
a signature story that's waiting to unfold.
When everything else has been shaken and down,
you lie like a seal on the edge of the crown.

### Outro

The ground is quiet, the ashes are cold,
but you are still sealed in the maker's own fold.
