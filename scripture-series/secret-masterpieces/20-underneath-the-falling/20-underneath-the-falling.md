# Underneath the Falling

**Song Number:** 20
**Target Date:** August 3, 2026
**Scripture Source:** Deuteronomy 33:27 _(personal reference — not published)_
**Genre:** Progressive Art Rock / Chamber Pop

---

### Verse 1

I watched the rafters cracking in the hall,
and felt the sudden pressure of the fall.
The floorboards parted like a frozen lake,
to swallow every promise we could make.

### Chorus

The gravity is pulling down the sky,
and tearing down the towers built so high.
But right beneath the bottom of the deep,
a pair of hands is waiting here to keep.

### Verse 2

We spend our lives in trying not to slide,
and building up the fences where we hide.
But in the rush of empty, open air,
the wind is whispered like a secret prayer.

### Chorus

The gravity is pulling down the sky,
and tearing down the towers built so high.
But right beneath the bottom of the deep,
a pair of hands is waiting here to keep.

### Bridge

This falling is a home we didn't plan,
beyond the careful logic of a man.
The oldest arms are woven in the space,
to turn the final drop into a grace.

### Final Chorus

So let the heavy architecture break,
and let the sleepy world begin to wake.
There is no ruin in the long descent,
for underneath, the oldest arms are bent.

### Outro

The ground has vanished but we do not fall,
the quiet dark is holding up us all.
