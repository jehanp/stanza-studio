The Sealed Fountain

Cinematic Folk / Chamber Pop

[Verse 1]
They built a wall around the water source,
and locked the gate along the garden's course.
No stranger's hand could reach the living spring,
or taste the secret nectar flowering.

[Verse 2]
A garden closed, a fountain sealed with stone,
where every fruit was planted, grown, and known.
The fragrance never scattered to the crowd,
but kept itself beneath a quiet shroud.

[Chorus]
You are a sealed fountain, hid from sight,
a garden locked and blooming through the night.
The spices and the myrrh are breathing still,
behind the gate that only love can fill.

[Verse 3]
Awake, north wind, and come across the ground,
and let the hidden perfumes scatter round.
Let him who holds the key come walk inside,
and taste the fruit no market ever buys.

[Chorus]
You are a sealed fountain, hid from sight,
a garden locked and blooming through the night.
The spices and the myrrh are breathing still,
behind the gate that only love can fill.

[Bridge]
Not everything that's precious should be shown,
some treasures only open to their own.
The rarest wine is kept in cellars deep,
and sacred things are guarded while they sleep.

[Final Chorus]
You are a sealed fountain, known by one,
a garden locked until the morning sun.
The fragrance fills the air for those who stay,
and every blossom opens for the day.

[Outro]
The key has turned, the garden breathes awake,
and living waters flow for beauty's sake.
