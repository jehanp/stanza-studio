# The Secret Stone

**Song Number:** 19
**Target Date:** July 20, 2026
**Scripture Source:** Revelation 2:17 _(personal reference — not published)_
**Genre:** Synth-Wave / Electropop

---

### Verse 1

The labels plastered on the old brick wall,
are spelling out the story of my fall.
They called me by the rumors and the lies,
and judged the shadows underneath my eyes.

### Pre-Chorus

I searched for bread inside the empty streets,
where everything is bought and then repeats.
But in the deepest silence of the night,
a hidden taste was turning into light.

### Chorus

And now my fingers touch a polished stone,
with secret letters meant for me alone.
The name they wrote is carved so deep and clear,
and wipes away the whispers of my fear.

### Verse 2

The neon signs are blinking in the rain,
and selling brand-new remedies for pain.
But none of them can touch the quiet place,
where truth is looking back into my face.

### Pre-Chorus

I searched for bread inside the empty streets,
where everything is bought and then repeats.
But in the deepest silence of the night,
a hidden taste was turning into light.

### Chorus

And now my fingers touch a polished stone,
with secret letters meant for me alone.
The name they wrote is carved so deep and clear,
and wipes away the whispers of my fear.

### Bridge

The hands that held the stars when they were spun,
have handed me the prize that has been won.
No thief can break the seal or steal the trace,
of what is written in this hidden space.

### Final Chorus

And now my hand is holding up the stone,
I am no longer walking on my own.
The secret name is carved so deep and clear,
and there is nothing left of all my fear.

### Outro

The white stone shines beneath the fading light,
and leads me safely through the neon night.
