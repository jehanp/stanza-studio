The Deep Calls Deep

Ambient Post-Rock / Shoegaze

[Verse 1]
The waterfalls are crashing overhead,
and every wave has pulled me from my bed.
The breakers and the billows take their turn,
and I am underneath them as they churn.

[Verse 2]
One depth is calling out to something more,
as if the ocean has a basement floor.
I thought the bottom was the lowest place,
but there's another level, another space.

[Chorus]
Deep calls to deep in the voice of the falls,
and something beneath me is answering calls.
The surface has lost me — I'm under the sound,
where what I was looking for finally is found.

[Verse 3]
By day a steadfast love is being sent,
and through the night a song without relent.
I'm praying to the rock beneath the sea,
and asking why the waves keep finding me.

[Chorus]
Deep calls to deep in the voice of the falls,
and something beneath me is answering calls.
The surface has lost me — I'm under the sound,
where what I was looking for finally is found.

[Bridge]
Why have you forgotten me? I ask the tide,
while enemies are mocking at my side.
But even drowning has a song to give,
and somewhere in the deep I learn to live.

[Final Chorus]
Deep calls to deep and I answer the pull,
the surface was empty, the depths are full.
The waterfalls crashed but they carried me down,
to where the living current can be found.

[Outro]
Below the noise, below the crashing white,
the deepest water holds the truest light.
