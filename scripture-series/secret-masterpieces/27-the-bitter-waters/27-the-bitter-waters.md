# The Bitter Waters

**Song Number:** 27
**Target Date:** November 9, 2026
**Scripture Source:** Exodus 15:23-25 _(personal reference — not published)_
**Genre:** Southern Gothic Blues / Swamp Rock

---

### Verse 1

I dragged my feet to where the water lay,
and hoped to wash the sticky dust away.
I bent my knees upon the muddy track,
and felt the burning sun upon my back.

### Verse 2

But when I drank, the poison made me choke,
and all the heavy words of anger woke.
The pool was stagnant, bitter to the taste,
and everything I loved had run to waste.

### Chorus

Oh, there is venom in the silent pool,
that breaks the spirit of a stubborn fool.
The old regrets are resting in the mud,
and flowing slowly through my heavy blood.

### Verse 3

Then someone took a piece of broken wood,
and threw it where the poisoned water stood.
A ripple started in the dark and grey,
and swept the poison of the years away.

### Chorus

Oh, there is venom in the silent pool,
that breaks the spirit of a stubborn fool.
The old regrets are resting in the mud,
and flowing slowly through my heavy blood.

### Bridge

The bitter branch has settled in the deep,
to wake the promise that was fast asleep.
The dark is clearing and the stream is bright,
and turning all the shadow into light.

### Final Chorus

Oh, now the water in the pool is sweet,
and washing over my exhausted feet.
The old regrets have vanished in the mud,
and peace is flowing through my heavy blood.

### Outro

Drink from the basin where the branch was cast,
the bitter waters have been healed at last.
