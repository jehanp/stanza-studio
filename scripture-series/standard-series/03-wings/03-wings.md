# Wings

**Song Number:** 03
**Target Date:** May 11, 2026
**Scripture Source:** Isaiah 40:28-31 _(personal reference — not published)_
**Genre:** Cinematic Pop / Anthemic

---

### Verse 1

I ran until my lungs gave out,
collapsed on someone else's kitchen floor.
The floorboards had a grain that looked like a river —
I traced it with my eyes and couldn't move anymore.

### Verse 2

They said push harder, dig deeper, try again,
but my arms forgot the language of reaching.
So I sat there in the quiet like a dare,
and the quiet wasn't empty — it was teaching.

### Pre-Chorus

I stopped running,
I stopped climbing,
I let the ground remember how to hold me.

### Chorus

And I felt it rise — something underneath,
a wind I didn't summon lifting higher than belief.
I'm not falling anymore, I'm not grinding in the rust —
I'm lifting off the pavement on a strength that isn't us.
Wings, wings —
I didn't grow them, but they're carrying me.

### Verse 3

The ones who sprint burn bright and crater fast,
but there's a slower kind of fire for the ones who choose to last.
I waited in the ashes like a fool or like a sage —
turns out it doesn't matter when you're ready to engage.

### Pre-Chorus

I stopped striving,
I stopped proving,
I let the stillness do the moving.

### Chorus

And I felt it rise — something underneath,
a wind I didn't summon lifting higher than belief.
I'm not crawling anymore, I'm not drowning in the flood —
I'm running down the highway on a pulse that's in my blood.
Wings, wings —
I didn't grow them, but they're carrying me.

### Bridge

They say the young won't tire,
they say the strong don't break —
but I've been both and I've been neither,
and I'm still here, wide awake.

### Final Chorus

And I feel it rise — something underneath,
the everlasting arms of a love beyond belief.
I'm not weary anymore, I'm not fainting on the road —
I'm soaring past the rooftops and I'm shedding every load.
Wings, wings —
I didn't earn them, but they're carrying me.
