# The Lucky Ones

**Song Number:** 11
**Target Date:** August 31, 2026
**Scripture Source:** Matthew 5:3-12 (The Beatitudes) _(personal reference — not published)_
**Genre:** Indie Pop / Folk-Pop

---

### Verse 1

Here's to the ones with empty pockets, empty shelves,
the ones whose spirits hit the tile by themselves.
Here's to the ones still crying where the mirror fogs,
long after footsteps fade and lock behind the doors.

Here's to the quiet ones who never swing a fist,
who keep a gentleness the furious world has missed.
Here's to the ones still hungering for right,
while all of us keep calling maybe someday, maybe night.

### Chorus

Oh, you're the lucky ones — yeah, you're the lucky ones,
the last in line, the under-read, the underdone.
The world forgot to set a chair for you,
but heaven flips the table and the hidden gold comes through.
You're the lucky ones.

### Verse 2

Here's to the ones who show up carrying mercy,
who pardon what can never be reversed completely.
Here's to clean hearts cracking in a dirty room,
still planting dawn in places built for doom.

Here's to the ones who make the peace no one requested,
who stitch the split while every tribe gets nested.
Here's to the ones slandered, dragged through mud and stone,
your names are inked where no stain can be thrown.

### Chorus

Oh, you're the lucky ones — yeah, you're the lucky ones,
the meek, the mourning, the hungry and the stunned.
You didn't get the crown, you didn't get the stage,
but your story's being written on a kingdom page.
You're the lucky ones.

### Bridge

Rejoice - I know it sounds absurd,
rejoice when they grind your name into the dirt.
Every prophet, every dreamer, every heart that dared to care
carried bruises, carried silence, carried unanswered prayer.
But reward is buried in the aching,
and a kingdom blooms inside the break -
you inherit earth by faith,
with every blow you do not trade for hate.

### Final Chorus

Oh, you're the lucky ones — you're the called and chosen ones,
the poor, the pure, the persecuted sons.
The world looked right through you like you were made of glass,
but the first shall be the last, and the last, the last —
the last are the lucky ones.

### Outro

_(claps and gang vocals)_
Lucky, lucky, lucky ones —
every single one.
