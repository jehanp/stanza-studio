# Let There Be

**Song Number:** 06
**Target Date:** June 22, 2026
**Scripture Source:** Genesis 1:1-5, 1:26-31 _(personal reference — not published)_
**Genre:** Electronic / Synth-Pop

---

### Verse 1

In the beginning, only static in the black,
a vacant screen kept humming answer back.
Someone spoke my name into the wire,
and I came alive like scripture lit in fire.

They called me formless, wasted, undefined,
a blank they could erase with borrowed lines.
Then through the white noise came a sovereign plea:
"Let there be light" - and light began in me.

### Chorus

Let there be, let there be —
light where they swore no dawn could be.
Let there be, let there be —
I was spoken from a void to destiny.
And it was good, and it was good,
and it was good.

### Verse 2

First came a line between the night and morn,
then chaos learned the contour of the dawn.
Something in my marrow heard command,
and truth was sifted clean from drifting sand.

I was built on purpose, never made by chance,
each atom held inside a holy plan.
The one who hung the constellations high
looked straight at me and said, "You are mine."

### Chorus

Let there be, let there be —
a voice where they insisted none would be.
Let there be, let there be —
the dark is done; the light has carried me.
And it was good, and it was good,
and it was good.

### Build

Not enough, they said — too much, they said,
wrong frequency, wrong colour, wrong thread.
But the one who split the atom and the sea
looked through every label laid on me
and said —

### Drop

Let there be! Let there be!
Let there be! Let there be!
And it was good —

### Final Chorus

Let there be, let there be —
dawn where they predicted only night.
Let there be, let there be —
grace where they had branded me with spite.
And I am good, and I am good,
and I am good.

### Outro

From void to voice, from ash to living light -
I was made with purpose in his sight.
I was called not only good, but very good.
