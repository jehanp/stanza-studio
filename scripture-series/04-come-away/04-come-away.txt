Come Away

Neo-Soul / Jazzy Pop

[Verse 1]

I built the wall in January,
stone by stone, alone and wary.
Said I'd never let another winter in —
I'd sleep alone and let the cold grow thin.

But there you were outside the window glass,
your voice like honey breaking through at last:
"The rain stopped hours ago, my love —
come see what bloomed in the field above."

[Pre-Chorus]

The fig tree's bearing heavy fruit,
the vine is lush down to the root.
I hear the birds return to sing —
a song lost somewhere in the spring.

[Chorus]

Come away, come away —
the winter's gone, the grey gave way.
Open up your bolted chest,
love has come to give you rest.
Come away, come away —
you've been hiding long enough today.

[Verse 2]

I pressed my ear against the brick
and heard the garden breathing thick.
The doves were paired along the wire
beneath a sky that thawed its fire.

You didn't knock, you didn't call,
you just stood steady through it all.
And something old inside my ribs
remembered all that the heart forgives.

[Chorus]

Come away, come away —
the winter's gone, the grey gave way.
Love was patient at your door,
you don't have to hide no more.
Come away, come away —
the flowers came while you were away.

[Bridge]

I was the frozen ground,
you were the stubborn bloom I found —
pushing through the frost of me
to let the smallest light run free.

[Final Chorus]

Come away, come away —
the season turned, I couldn't stay.
I'm stepping out beyond the wall I've made —
love was never far, love never strayed.
Come away, come away —
I think I'm ready for the day.

[Vamp]

Come away... come away...
The door is open now — the door is open.
Come away... come away...

[Outro]

The rain is over, the rain is gone —
the morning smells like moving on.
